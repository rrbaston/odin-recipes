Crunchy Frog

Description

Our quintessential chocolate, the Crunchy Frog.

You can see above our beloved owner, Mr. Milton, explaining passionately to our clients what makes this our finest chocolate. Now the secret to this delight is revealed!

We use the finest baby frogs, dew picked and flown from Iraq, cleansed in finest quality spring water, lightly killed, and then sealed in a succulent Swiss quintuple smooth treble cream milk chocolate envelope and lovingly frosted with glucose.

Ingredients

  • Baby frogs
  • Spring water
  • Sucullent Swiss quintuple smooth treble cream milk chocolate
  • Glucose frosting
